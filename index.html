HISTORY OF BHARAT SEVASASHRAM SANGHA

Bharat Sevashram Sangha is a Hindu religious and spiritual organisation focused on humanitarian work.
It was founded in 1917 by the illustrious patriot saint Acharya Srimat Swami Pranavananda Maharaj.
It is purely a philanthropic and charitable organization with non-sectarian, non-communal and non-political character and outlook.
